The Design Pipeline

How we take your ideas from concept to physical reality.

01

Consultation & Assets

We analyze photographs, maps, and drawings to determine optimal scale, style, and color matching.

02

Digital Engineering

Our team builds a complete 3D schematic model, running structural integrity and part optimization analysis.

03

Approval

Once the digital design is complete, we provide high-quality renders from a variety of angles to ensure that every last part is to your liking.

04

Sourcing

We source premium bricks and apply custom UV graphics to ensure your final sets are the highest quality possible. We also assemble the initial evaluation build.

05

Packaging & UX

We consider User Experience at every step. From printed instruction booklets to bespoke custom boxes, we ensure that every impression the user has is cohesive and memorable.

06

Deployment & Fulfillment

Whether you need a single massive showroom display or 5,000 retail-ready wholesale sets, we manage the logistics to ensure your project arrives perfectly.

📐

Miniature Scale Replicas

Modeling of historic landmarks, corporate headquarters, and local architecture. We integrate custom-printed parts and precise color matching to ensure your architectural vision is realized with exact fidelity.

⚙️

CAD Modeling

We provide comprehensive 3D visualization, including exploded views and assembly sequences. This allows you to review the internal structural integrity and aesthetic profile before we move to production.

📖

Professional Instructions

We produce high-quality, step-by-step assembly guides. Whether digital PDFs or professionally printed booklets, our instructions ensure your customers have a seamless, frustration-free building experience.

📦

Custom Packaging

Completely bespoke, six-sided packaging. We design custom die-lines to fit your set perfectly, ensuring a premium unboxing experience with full-bleed graphics and professional-grade branding that makes your product shelf-ready.

🖨️

Custom-Printed Parts

We utilize industrial UV printing to apply high-resolution graphics directly onto elements. From corporate logos and wayfinding signage to complex textural patterns, we elevate standard parts into unique, custom-branded assets.
